Who we are

We are locally owned business based out of DC metro area with a passion for Ice Cream.

We started Ice Ice Baby to share our passion with others and encourage people to explore the pursuits that make them as happy as ice cream makes us. We have learned so much from our team and our constant pursuit to make the best ice cream. We hope that our flavors give you a reason to smile and jump for joy!


Our journey begin with the first store in PG Plaza and now spanned over two locations in Wheaton and now opening a new location in Springfield.
