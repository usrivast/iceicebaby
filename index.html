Who we are

We are locally owned business based out of Greater Washington metropolitan area with a passion for Ice Cream, Real fruit smoothies and natural flavor Snowballs

We started Ice Ice Baby to share our passion with others and encourage people to explore the pursuits that make them as happy as ice cream makes us. We have learned so much from our team and our constant pursuit to make the best smoothies from real fruits and free from artificial flavor. We hope that our flavors give you a reason to smile and jump for joy!


Our journey started with the first push cart store in 1991 in PG Plaza, and quickly expanding to Ballston commons mall, Montgomery mall and Greenbelt. Soon after we opened a store in Wheaton mall. Currently we are operating stores in PG Plaza, MD and Springfield Town center, VA
